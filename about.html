Novia Hoyte started out as a Mathematics/Criminal Justice major with plans to attend a top law school and become a prosecutor. As a part of her required coursework, Novia took several coding courses including C++, Java, and MySQL. These courses planted the seed of interest that set Novia onto an alternate path.

Upon finishing her B.S. in Mathematics and B.A. in Criminal Jutice in 2015, she decided to enroll in graduate school as an Information Technology major, forgoeing her prior plans to attend law school.

After completing her M.S. in Information Technology, Novia was confident that she made the right choice! She loves to learn and enjoys the ever-changing flow of technology.
